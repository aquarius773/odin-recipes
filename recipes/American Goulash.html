American Goulash

Description

A delicious recipe made with macaronic elbow pasta with ground beef cooked in the same pot and a red sauce made of tomatoes and red wine.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 28oz. can diced tomatoes
  • 1 15oz. can tomato sauce
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 whole bay leaves (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni (about 1/2 lb.)
  • salt to taste
